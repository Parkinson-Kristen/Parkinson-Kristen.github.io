Fisherman's Delight

by Tom Trout

If you are looking for a perfect fishing getaway to commune with nature, then Fish Haven is the place for you. From the fresh water trout to the myriad of scenic hikes thoughout the hill, there is plenty to keep a busy family busy or just settle back for a nice relaxing weekend. If you are looking for a scenic drive, be sure to check out Bear Lake. Easy access to National Forests as well. See you soon! We'll save you a fish.
