A TOUCH OF HEAVEN

At Temple Inn Suites there are many options

Temple Inn & Suites are located near the following temples:

Snowflake Arizona

The Snowflake Arizona temple was the 2nd temple for Arizona. Members of the area were thrilled to get a temple closer to home. The closest temple (when announced) was the Mesa Arizona temple, 3+ hour drive away. The temple can been seen from miles away as it sits on a small hill perfectly situated to be seen from miles around.

Gilbert Arizona

The Gilbert Arizona temple was the 2nd temple announced for the Phoenix area. With the growth of the east valley, a temple was eagerly prayed for.

Tucson Arizona

The Tucson Arizona temple was one of the most contested temples in Arizona. Residents of the area fought the temple placement for years, but today, with home prices around the temple grounds and the beauty of the surrounding, hopefully residents are a little more accepting.

and COMING SOON to Mesa Arizona

The Mesa Arizona temple is undergoing an exciting transformation. Around 20 acres are under renovation. Residents of Arizona are eagerly awaiting it's completion and the return of the Easter Pagent and Christmas lights!
