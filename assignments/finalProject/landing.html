Temple Inn & Suites Lobby

Our lobby is both inviting and relaxing. Free access to changing rooms and restroom facilities for temple patrons who need to change to and from church clothing. Please enjoy free WiFi high speed internet thoughout our facility.

Guest Rooms

We offer a variety of room sizes. Additionally, we have several long-term stay suites for full-time temple missionaries that boast a kitchenette in addition to our many other services. Rooms include Smart TV's, Private ensuite restrooms, ironing board. Free parking is included with your stay - please see reception for parking validation. Please see our "services" directory for more information.

Reception Hall

Out wedding reception hall can be easily customized with a simple catering menue and has kitchen access for food preparation and service. We also offer referals to a variety of catering companies who offer special rates for guests of Temple Inn Suites. Please contact us for further details.
