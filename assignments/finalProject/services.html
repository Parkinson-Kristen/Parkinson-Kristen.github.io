Services we offer

DAY CARE

Short term day care can be provide as an additional fee service when you book your stay with us. Please contact us for additional information and references. Day care is for the children of temple patrons. Our day care room space is limited. 4-hour maximum. Please plan in advance for this service


FAMILY HISTORY RESERCH

Whether you are reserving research time between sessions or looking to find additional branches in your family tree, our knowledgable staff can help you with your reseach. We have access to Family Tree and The Church of Jesus Christ's databases as well as Zoom room conferencing available.


CATERING REFERALS

Are you looking to book a venue for a wedding reception or youth conference luncheon? We are here to help!
